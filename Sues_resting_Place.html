About Sue’s Headstone

I wanted Sue’s headstone to be a source of inspiration and light for those who visit her and spent a long time working on ideas realising that it couldn’t be perfect but it would be my best.

Bob Dylan was one of Sue’s favourite singer song writers and the text ‘May you build a ladder to the stars” is taken from the song “Forever Young”, one of her favourite Dylan songs

The text down the side of the headstone reads ‘The sun shines brighter because you were here”. Sue was a source of inspiration to so many.

The central ceramic represents Sue with images of her work and images which inspired her . This was selected by friends from a series of six variations. The central ceramic and ceramic bronze stars are set into the marble of the headstone.

An ex pupil Graham Baker, now an accomplished stone mason, was responsible for the final realisation of the design and made the whole design possible.

On the side of the headstone is a QR code set into the stone encapsulated in glass by which visitors can be directed straight to this website.
